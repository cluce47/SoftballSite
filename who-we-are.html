Who We Are

About Our League

South Ottawa Softball is a community-driven organization dedicated to bringing adults together through the love of softball. We offer a welcoming and inclusive environment for players of all skill levels, where fun, teamwork, and personal growth take center stage.

Our programs are purely recreational, and focuses on building friendships and creating enjoyable experiences on the field. With passionate organizers and well-maintained facilities, we provide the perfect setting for players to connect and have fun.

Our Roots

Established over a decade ago, our league has grown from a small group of friends to a vibrant community. We remain committed to nurturing young talent, promoting teamwork, and creating an inclusive atmosphere where every member can thrive.

Whether it's organizing community fundraisers or hosting friendly exhibition games, we believe in giving back and building strong connections. We celebrate each player's journey and look forward to new chapters in our ongoing story.
